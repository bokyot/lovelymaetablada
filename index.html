about myself

My name is Lovely Mae Tablada. I am Graduate in Bachelor in Information and Technology degree. I learn different programming languages like Java, PHP, Python,C#, Ionic Angular in my course and also through self study, and watching youtube videos. The experience of being a programmer in group activity also help develop my knowledge. I prefer front end development since I'm more on designing website and application.

During my college years I can say that I am a responsible and a hard-working student. Moreover, being an ambivert person, I have just few friends that i can trust truly. I enjoy my time at school: it is really nice to study, My classmates are friendly and ready to help the atmosphere makes me want to go there every time. I like to receive and deal with challenging tasks but also gets frustrated if i cannot solve it. I am a very enthusiastic student and I think this is a strong point of mine.

My friends say that I am a very funny and an interesting girl with a good sense of humor. I actually being talkative infront of my friends and to those people I'm comfortable but, I'm still friendly eventhough i don't speak a lot with new people. I believe that friendship is one of the most important values in human life. We exchange new ideas, find many interesting things about each other and experience new things. I appreciate friendship and people who surround me.

Meeting new people who support all my efforts to grow and develop my skills not only in the subjects but also in my hobbies gives me a lot of energy. Many of those friends who surround me now I met on the street while doing my hobby. I’m in love with Camping, so the thing I love doing so much became even more enjoyable. I decided to take adventures and to take a rest in a stressful life. I feel happiness inside seeing how beatiful the earth is going in adventure cleanse my soul and help me become more calm and appreciate things. In this way, I encourage myself to do good job in my life even if I'm not good enough with other things I want my future me to enjoy things more freely with the path i wanted to take and that is when I will say that my life is a goal.

The goal I’m currently striving for is to make this world a better place to live in by starting the changes with myself. Sure, I’ve had bad experiences in my life too, but this is exactly what made me the way I am now: grateful, full of love, with a desire to study well because it will help me become a successful person in future and have a high quality of life. I believe that it is manifesting day by day and I feel even more responsible for what I do and my future. With all I already have, I know that I’m on the right path and I will do my best to inspire others to live the way they feel like living as well. I have a lot of problems but i don't let that eat my life becoming a sad and stressful person. I always tell myself to become optimistic since everything has a reason and liife is too short to focus in bad things.
